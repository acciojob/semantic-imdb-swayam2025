Summary

The 1993 Bollywood blockbuster Tirangaa is a patriotic action-drama directed by Mehul Kumar, starring Raaj Kumar as Brigadier Suryadev Singh and Nana Patekar as Police Inspector Shivajirao Wagle. They team up to thwart a terrorist named Pralaynath Gundaswamy, who plans to destroy India on Independence Day using stolen missiles.
